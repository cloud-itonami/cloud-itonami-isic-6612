Security and commodity contracts brokerage

ISIC Rev.5 6612 • Open Business Blueprint

An open-source brokerage actor for client-account intake, suitability screening, and trade execution—governed by an independent verification layer that verifies every proposal against spec-cited jurisdiction requirements, conflict-of-interest checks, and risk-tolerance matching rules. Trade execution is never autonomous; a human broker always retains final authority. Built for broker-dealers, cooperatives, and community investment programs seeking transparent, audited, and governed order management.
